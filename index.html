About Green Nexus Advisory

Green Nexus Advisory is a Tirana-based expert collective specialized in strategic consulting at the intersection of governance, environment, and energy. Founded by senior adviser Dr. Elton Qendro, with over 20 years of experience in national and regional development, GNA offers tailored expertise to governments, donors, NGOs, and academic institutions.

We bring deep, applied knowledge in policy development, institutional analysis, climate security, and decentralized governance, with a focus on delivering measurable results, facilitating inclusive processes, and strengthening public institutions across multiple sectors.

Evidence-based • Inclusive • Results-oriented

Mission

To empower institutions, communities, and development partners with practical, high-quality advisory services that advance sustainability, resilience, and effective governance.

We bridge knowledge and practice across Albania, the Western Balkans, and beyond.
