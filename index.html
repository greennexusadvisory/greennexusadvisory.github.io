Who we are

Green Nexus Advisory is a Tirana-based expert collective providing strategic consulting at the intersection of governance, environment, and energy. Founded by senior adviser Dr. Elton Qendro (20+ years of experience in national and regional development), GNA supports governments, donors, NGOs, and academic institutions.

What defines GNA

  • Applied policy expertise: policy development, institutional analysis, and decentralized governance
  • Climate security and risk governance: adaptation, environmental crime, and resilience planning
  • Inclusive processes: multi-stakeholder engagement and facilitation
  • Measurable results: evidence-driven recommendations and implementable pathways

Mission statement

Green Nexus Advisory empowers institutions, communities, and development partners to design, implement, and evaluate transformative policies in environmental governance, energy transition, and local development. We bridge knowledge and practice to build sustainable, just, and resilient systems in Albania, the Western Balkans, and beyond.

Services offered

Core services structured around five practice areas (tailored to project needs and donor requirements).

Environment & Climate Governance

  • Environmental policy design and reform
  • EU environmental directives compliance (Chapter 27)
  • Climate adaptation, environmental crime, and risk governance
  • Strategic support for Aarhus and Espoo Convention implementation

Energy Policy & Transition Planning

  • National Energy & Climate Plan (NECP) evaluation
  • Renewable energy policy support
  • Socio-environmental assessment of hydropower development
  • Public consultation and stakeholder engagement in energy planning

Institutional Capacity & Local Governance

  • Decentralization policy support and fiscal reform
  • Mining royalty and local finance strategies
  • Municipal service delivery improvement
  • Capacity building and training for public officials

Research, Evaluation & Impact Studies

  • Baseline, midline, and impact evaluations
  • Stakeholder analysis and perception studies
  • EU PRAG-compliant procurement & project appraisal
  • Monitoring frameworks and performance reviews

Strategic Planning & Donor Engagement

  • Policy roadmaps and action plans
  • Multi-stakeholder dialogue facilitation
  • Project concept notes, TORs, funding strategies
  • Regional and cross-border programming

Projects

Past experience highlights (examples—expand with dates, partners, and outputs as you wish).

  • Environmental Governance (Albania): Led multi-year initiatives on environmental governance, environmental crime, Aarhus Convention implementation, and impact assessment frameworks.
  • Energy & Climate Plan Review (GIZ, 2024): Evaluated Albania’s NECP alignment with EU goals; identified policy gaps and opportunities for stakeholder participation.
  • Impact Evaluation Expert (ADF / World Bank): Led evaluation of the Rural Roads Project and 100 Villages Program, delivering data-driven policy recommendations.
  • Environmental Crime Strategy: Co-authored national strategy and built institutional capacities for environmental enforcement.
  • Decentralization Reform (UNDP Iraq / EU): Institutional gap analysis and recommendations for local governance and agriculture reforms.
  • Stakeholder Mapping (IUCN Vjosa River): Mapped institutional and community stakeholders across 9 municipalities in the Vjosa basin.
  • Climate Adaptation & DRR (Regional): Designed participatory resilience planning tools in Albania and the Western Balkans.
  • Regional programming: Cross-border and multi-actor approaches aligned with donor requirements and public-sector implementation realities.
